About TOYZY

TOYZY is not just a printer, it's a learning experience. Built under the Hack Club Highway program, it's a hands-on intro to open-source hardware. This printer helped us dive into firmware flashing, motion control, 3D modeling, and real-world problem solving — using Arduino + RAMPS and Marlin firmware.

FAQ – What people ask about TOYZY

TOYZY runs on Marlin firmware — the most popular open-source 3D printer firmware used by DIY and commercial printers.
It uses an Arduino Mega 2560 + RAMPS 1.4 board to control all the motors, heaters, and LCD. Super flexible and mod-friendly!
Yup! TOYZY's hotend and heated bed are 24V, which makes it totally ready for PLA, PETG, and even flexible filament if you're careful.
